Digital marketing refers to the use of digital channels, platforms, and technologies to promote and sell products or services. It encompasses a wide range of tactics such as search engine optimization (SEO), social media marketing, email marketing, content creation, pay-per-click advertising (PPC), and influencer partnerships. Unlike traditional marketing, digital marketing allows businesses to reach a global audience in real-time, measure performance with precision, and tailor messages to specific demographics. Its data-driven nature helps marketers understand customer behavior, improve engagement, and optimize return on investment (ROI). As technology continues to evolve, digital marketing remains a critical strategy for businesses looking to grow in the digital age. .
